About

Smart WI-FI thermostats have moved well beyond the function they were originally designed for;
namely,controlling heating and cooling comfort in buildings.

  • * They are now also learning form occupant behaviours and permit occupants to control their comfort remotely.

  • * This project seeks to go beyound this state of the art by utilizing smart Wi-Fi thermostat data in residences to develop dynamic predictive models for room temperature.
